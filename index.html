Privacy Architecture

Data Vault & Security Protocols

1. AR Processing & Biometrics: The AR Drape & Fit engine utilizes local browser APIs (WebRTC). Your camera feed is processed strictly on your device. We do not transmit, record, or store any facial recognition data or live video streams on our servers.

2. Supabase Encryption: User dimensions, aesthetic preferences, and order histories are stored in our decentralized Supabase architecture. All data is protected via Row Level Security (RLS), meaning no external entity can query your profile matrix.

3. Payment Gateway: We use Razorpay for secure checkout. Aesthetica does not log, process, or store credit card numbers or UPI PINs. All financial nodes are AES-256 encrypted end-to-end.

4. Affiliate Analytics: Interaction with "Cheapest Finds" via external links may drop anonymous tracking cookies strictly to attribute creator bounties. No personal identity is attached to these outbound clicks.
